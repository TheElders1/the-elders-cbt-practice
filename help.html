Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a quiz?

From the Home page, enter your full name, select your department from the dropdown menu, and then choose your specific course. Once all fields are filled, click the "Start Quiz" button.

How are the quizzes structured?

Each course is divided into two segments of 50 questions each. You can choose which segment you want to practice. The quiz is timed to help you practice under pressure.

Can I review my answers?

Yes. After you submit your quiz, you will see a results screen with your score. On this screen, there is a "Review Answers" button that will show you a detailed breakdown of every question, your answer, and the correct answer.

The quiz for my course isn't loading. What should I do?

Please double-check that you have selected the correct department and course name. If it still doesn't load, the quiz data for that specific course might not be available yet. Please contact us to let us know.
