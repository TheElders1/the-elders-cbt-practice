How Can We Help?

Find answers to common questions below.

Go to the Home page, enter your full name (at least 6 characters), select your department, and then choose your specific course from the dropdown menus. Click the "Start Quiz" button to proceed.

Each course is divided into two segments of 50 questions each, covering different aspects of the curriculum. The quiz is timed for 25 minutes to simulate real exam conditions and help you practice managing your time effectively.

Your performance is automatically tracked and stored locally on your device. You can view detailed analytics including your progress trends, weak areas, achievements, and study streaks on your personal dashboard. Click "View Dashboard" from the welcome message or visit the dashboard directly.

XP (Experience Points) are earned based on your quiz performance. You get 10 XP per correct answer, plus bonuses for perfect scores (100 XP) and speed completion (50 XP). Achievements are unlocked for various milestones like completing your first quiz, maintaining study streaks, or reaching certain levels. Your level increases every 1000 XP!

The system tracks questions you get wrong and prioritizes them in future quizzes. Up to 30% of your next quiz will focus on your weak areas, helping you improve faster. You can also view your weak areas on the dashboard to see which topics need more attention.
